As mentioned elsewhere in this wiki, I have a blog.

On said blog, I've done some thinking about tags.

So here is a hierarchy of my tags:

  • SciTexh: posts about science and technology.
    • software: that which has consumed most of my life.
      • debugging: my favorite part of software development. I get to be a detective.
      • C: The C language.
      • Shell: Shell scripting.
    • Multicast: a technical subject I've been involved with since 2005.
    • sockets: no, not light bulb receptacles, the Berkley networking API.
    • TCP: the protocol.
    • UDP: the protocol.
  • humor: my sense of humor has been called "indiscriminate", even "sick". What can I say? I love to laugh.
  • Me: posts which I feel say something notable about who and what kind of person I am.
    • rants: everybody likes to complain, right? The difference is that my rants are well-reasoned and insightful.
    • thinking: of minimal interest to strangers. I like to think out loud - it helps me to clarify my reasoning. Rather than drawing stares in public places talking to myself, I'll try to do it here.
  • Social Media: everybody else blogs about social media, why can't I?
    • blogging: blogging about blogging. How meta is that?
    • LinkedIn: Facebook for business? Or something more? (Hard to be less...)
  • email: blogging about email.
  • tips: I sometimes have useful ideas.
